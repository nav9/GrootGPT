About

This program was created by Nav, as an attempt to see how much of a program could be created by prompting OpenAI's Chat GPT. Yes, the entire HTML and JavaScript on this website was generated by Chat GPT. The profile icons were generated via Stable Diffusion Online and scaled in GIMP manually. The program does not use GPT to generate outputs. GPT was only used to build and refine the code that runs this page. A trivial sentiment-analysis function is present, and will cause Groot to reply with emoticons. For example, if you use a sentence containing words like "happy", "love", or "sad" (let's see if you can guess which other words produce emoticons).

A side-quest of this program is to raise awareness about understanding that the human eye was not evolved to handle long hours of staring at a screen. The inner and outer eye muscles get tired and pain. That pain is your eyes screaming at you, begging for rest.

  • So after 20 minutes of screentime, simply close your eyes and wait until the strain reduces.
  • Also, ensure you get 8 hours uninterrupted sleep each night to let the eye muscles heal.
  • Don't forget to consume a well balanced diet of nutritious, well-cooked food.
Companies, friends and family need to build a supportive environment to help people get proper sleep and avoid over-exerting themselves and suffering a burnout. Those who have been through a burnout need support to recover. Let's build a healthy world.
