About

This program was created entirely by prompting OpenAI's Chat GPT. Only the profile icon files were added manually. A side-quest of this program is to raise awareness about The Real Cure For Eye Strain. Rather than falling for marketing gimmicks that promise to cure or reduce eye strain, you need to understand that the human eye was not evolved to handle long hours of staring at a screen. The inner and outer eye muscles get tired and pain. That pain is your eyes screaming at you, begging for rest. So after 20 minutes of screentime, simply close your eyes and wait until the strain reduces. Also, ensure you get 8 hours uninterrupted sleep each night to let the eye muscles heal. Don't forget to consume a well balanced diet of nutritious, well-cooked food.
